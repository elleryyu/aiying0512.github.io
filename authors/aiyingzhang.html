Research Interests

My research interests span mental health data science, focusing on statistical and computational approaches leveraging multimodal imaging and genetics to understand typical and atypical brain development.

  • Graphical Models (Directed/Undirected)
  • Machine Learning in Neuroscience
  • Multimodal Fusion
  • Data types: MRI, DTI, fMRI, MEG, SNP, DNA methylation
  • Psychiatric Diseases: Schizophrenia, Alzheimer's disease, OCD, etc.
